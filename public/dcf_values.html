A Global Community for Human-Centric Data Sovereignty, Sovereign AI, and Cyber Technology for Human Good

AI Is Changing Everything — But Who Is It Changing For?

In the age of artificial intelligence, the very definition of what it means to be human is being rewritten. We are called to help chart a course for humanity where technology is advanced in authentic service of the human family.

Technology is evolving faster than society's values. Around the world, communities are asking: Can AI reflect our shared humanity, or will it divide us further? We believe in a future where every person, culture, and faith has a seat at the table — and sovereignty over their own data and digital identity.

Like every advancement of the human spirit, AI can be an incredible gift to improve the lives of billions and find solutions to urgent global challenges. Conversely, it can also accelerate inequality and discrimination, eroding the human and ethical dimension from our societies.

Technology can and must lift up humanity, and humanity must not become subjugated to technology

Together, We Can Shape an AI Future That Honors Every Human Value

We are a constellation of nonprofits building a commons of donors, experts, technologists, academia, researchers and research institutes, governmental leaders and regulators, individuals, humanitarian organizations, and innovators for a world where technology serves humanity — not the other way around.

Let's reclaim our digital sovereignty, honor our diversity, and co-create an ethical foundation for AI and the cyberspace that uplifts all people. This model engages everyone throughout the Global North, South, East and West to bring the community together from everywhere and embrace the best of the values of all cultures and all faiths, maintaining cultural histories and diversities in the programming of the digital space.

We were formed with the inspiration of the values from Catholic Social Teaching with the support of the Vatican to integrate the values of all faiths and humankind into cyber technology and AI for the betterment of humankind.

DCF created an international platform that serves as a tool at the service of the Catholic Church in advancing its mission on ethical technology. We are building a constellation of organizations and individuals with globally recognized expertise to create a 'cyber commons' as a bridge for ethical, responsible and human-centered technology inspired by Catholic Social teachings and principles shared across all faiths.

We stand at a critical juncture in human history. The decisions we make today about AI and technology will shape the future for generations to come.

If we do nothing, the future will be written by a few — not for the many.
If we act together, the future can be written by all of us, for all of us.

The goal is creating an interactive global community to support and together build values in AI and be a vehicle to provide everyone access to their own digital sovereignty and technology, foundational for the good of humanity and sustainable peace.
