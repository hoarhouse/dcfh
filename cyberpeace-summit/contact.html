About Domus Communis Foundation Hungary

Our Organization

The Domus Communis Foundation Hungary is the organizing body behind the Budapest Digital Peace Summit. We are committed to promoting digital sovereignty, ethical AI development, and human-centered technology that serves the common good. Our foundation works to bridge the gap between technological innovation and human dignity, ensuring that digital transformation serves all of humanity.
