About Domus Communis Foundation Hungary

Learn more about the organization behind the Global Digital & AI Peace Summit and our commitment to ending digital warfare.

The Domus Communis Foundation Hungary is the organizing body behind the Global Digital & AI Peace Summit. We are committed to promoting digital & AI peace, ending cyber warfare, and developing technology that serves humanity's highest aspirations. Our foundation works to bridge the gap between technological innovation and human dignity, ensuring that digital transformation creates a more peaceful and just world.
