Ending Digital Warfare, Building Digital Peace

For nearly a decade, the world has watched the tragic conflict between Russia and Ukraine—the first conflict in human history to take place at scale in both physical and digital dimensions. This summit convenes 100-300 global leaders in technology, faith, government, and academia to work together toward ceasing digital warfare in the current conflict and preventing future digital escalation.

Digital Warfare Reality

Cyber misinformation, disruption of critical human services, and cyber weapons have transformed modern conflict. We must address these urgent threats to global security and civilian safety.

AI-Driven Autonomous Weapons

The rise of AI-powered autonomous weapons systems poses unprecedented risks to humanity. This summit addresses the urgent need for international cooperation and ethical boundaries.

Path to Digital Peace

Moving beyond condemnation to constructive dialogue, concrete peace initiatives, and binding international agreements that prevent digital warfare escalation.

Digital Peace Summit Themes

01

Ceasing Digital Warfare

Addressing the immediate crisis: how to end cyber warfare in Ukraine-Russia conflict and establish international norms to prevent future digital escalation.

  • Cyber misinformation & propaganda
  • Critical infrastructure attacks
  • International digital warfare law
  • Civilian protection in cyberspace
02

AI-Autonomous Weapons

Preventing the proliferation of AI-driven autonomous weapons systems and establishing global agreements for responsible AI in military applications.

  • Autonomous weapons systems
  • AI-powered surveillance
  • Military AI ethics
  • Human oversight requirements
03

Digital Peace Framework

Building institutional mechanisms for digital peace, including early warning systems, diplomatic protocols, and enforcement mechanisms for digital warfare prevention.

  • Digital peace treaties
  • Cyber diplomacy protocols
  • Early warning systems
  • International enforcement

Key Summit Initiatives

The Budapest Appeal

A formal declaration calling for the immediate cessation of digital warfare in the Ukraine-Russia conflict and the establishment of international norms preventing future cyber warfare escalation.

Tree of Peace Ceremony

A symbolic tree from the Vatican Gardens will be planted at Szabadság tér (Liberty Square), representing our commitment to growing digital peace from the seeds of dialogue and cooperation.

Budapest Declaration

The Budapest Declaration on Digital Peace & Sovereign AI will establish binding principles for responsible AI development and deployment in military and civilian contexts.

This summit follows the format of high-level diplomatic gatherings like the Munich Security Conference and Bilderberg meetings—emphasizing discretion, deep dialogue, and actionable outcomes with a 10 million euro budget for world-class production.
