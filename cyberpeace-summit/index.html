Shaping the Future of Technology & Human Dignity

The Budapest Digital Peace Summit brings together global leaders in technology, ethics, faith, and policy to address the most pressing questions of our digital age: How do we build technology that serves humanity? How do we protect human dignity in an AI-driven world?

A Global Conversation

Convening tech innovators, Catholic leaders, policymakers, and civil society to forge a new path forward for ethical AI and digital sovereignty.

Rooted in Values

Grounded in Catholic social teaching and universal human rights, promoting technology that respects human dignity and the common good.

Action-Oriented

Moving beyond dialogue to concrete commitments, partnerships, and initiatives that will shape the future of technology governance.

Summit Themes

01

AI Ethics & Governance

Exploring frameworks for responsible AI development rooted in human dignity, transparency, and accountability. How do we ensure AI serves the common good?

  • AI bias and fairness
  • Algorithmic transparency
  • Global AI governance frameworks
  • Faith perspectives on AI ethics
02

Digital Sovereignty

Reclaiming control over our digital lives, data, and infrastructure. Building systems that empower individuals and communities rather than extract from them.

  • Data ownership and privacy
  • Decentralized infrastructure
  • Digital rights and freedoms
  • Alternatives to surveillance capitalism
03

Technology & Human Dignity

Ensuring technology enhances rather than diminishes what it means to be human. Creating digital spaces that foster genuine connection, creativity, and flourishing.

  • Social media and mental health
  • Digital addiction and wellbeing
  • Technology and human relationships
  • Faith in the digital age
