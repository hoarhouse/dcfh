Partnership for Digital Peace

The Global Digital Peace Summit is made possible through partnerships with organizations committed to ending digital warfare, preventing autonomous weapons proliferation, and establishing international frameworks for cyber peace. Together, we're working to cease current conflicts and prevent future digital escalation.

DCF Hungary Logo

Domus Communis Foundation

Lead Organizing Partner

The Domus Communis Foundation is a constellation of non-profit organizations dedicated to human-centric, ethical, value-based, safe, Sovereign AI and Data Sovereignty. As the organizing partner, DCF brings together global leaders to advance digital peace and end cyber warfare for the common good.
