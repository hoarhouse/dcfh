Why Budapest?

Budapest, the magnificent "Pearl of the Danube," serves as the perfect backdrop for discussions on digital & AI peace. This historic city has long been a bridge between East and West, embodying the spirit of dialogue and cooperation that defines our summit's mission.

Rich Heritage

A city with over 1,000 years of history, featuring stunning architecture from Roman times through the Habsburg era to modern innovation.

Catholic Tradition

Rich Catholic heritage with strong Vatican representation through Cardinal Silvano M. Tomasi, leading interfaith dialogue on technology and ethics.

Tech Innovation

Hungary's thriving technology sector makes Budapest a hub for digital innovation while maintaining strong values of human dignity.

Cultural Bridge

Historically connecting Eastern and Western traditions, Budapest embodies the collaborative spirit essential for digital & AI peace.

Heroes' Square: A Historic Gathering Place

Adjacent to the Venue To Be Determined, Heroes' Square (Hősök tere) serves as the magnificent outdoor ceremonial space for the summit's symbolic peace gestures.

Strategic Location

Heroes' Square sits directly in front of the Venue To Be Determined, creating a seamless connection between indoor diplomatic sessions and outdoor ceremonial events. This UNESCO World Heritage site provides an inspiring backdrop just steps from the main summit venue.

Historical Significance

Since 1896, Heroes' Square has served as Budapest's premier gathering place for historic moments. The Millennium Monument and surrounding colonnades honor Hungary's thousand-year history, making it the perfect setting to inaugurate a new era of digital & AI peace.

Tree of Religions Ceremony

The square's vast open space and City Park setting provide the ideal canvas for the summit's signature drone light performance. Hundreds of synchronized drones will create a luminous "Tree of Religions" above the historic square, symbolizing interfaith unity in the digital age.

Peaceful Park Setting

Adjacent City Park (Városliget) offers tranquil green spaces for informal dialogues and contemplation. The park's serene environment, with its lakes and century-old trees, provides natural sanctuary for summit participants between intensive diplomatic sessions.

Symbolic Convergence

Heroes' Square represents the convergence of past and future—where monuments to historical heroes meet cutting-edge drone technology to create new symbols of peace. This juxtaposition of tradition and innovation perfectly embodies the summit's mission to honor timeless values while embracing technological progress for humanity's benefit.

Getting to Budapest

Budapest is easily accessible from anywhere in the world with excellent transportation connections and a beautiful, walkable city center.

By Air

Budapest Ferenc Liszt International Airport (BUD) is just 15 minutes from the city center. Major airlines serve Budapest with direct flights from most European capitals and many international destinations.

Airport Transfer

Multiple transport options: dedicated airport shuttle (100E), taxi services, ride-sharing, and car rental. The 100E bus connects directly to city center in 30-45 minutes.

Public Transport

Excellent metro, tram, and bus system. Purchase a 3-day travel card for convenient access to the Venue To Be Determined and additional summit locations. Metro lines connect major attractions and business districts efficiently.

Getting Around

Walking-friendly city center, bike-sharing program, and reliable taxi services. Many summit locations accessible on foot from central accommodation areas.
