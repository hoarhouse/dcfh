Why Now?

We stand at a pivotal moment in human history. Artificial intelligence is reshaping every aspect of society‚Äîfrom how we work and learn to how we relate to one another and understand ourselves.

Digital Warfare Crisis

The Russia-Ukraine conflict has demonstrated how cyber weapons, AI-driven disinformation, and attacks on digital infrastructure have transformed warfare. For the first time in history, we are witnessing a war fought simultaneously in both physical and digital dimensions at scale.

AI-Powered Autonomous Weapons

The deployment of artificial intelligence in military systems raises unprecedented ethical concerns. Autonomous weapons that can select and engage targets without human intervention threaten to fundamentally alter the nature of armed conflict and challenge basic principles of human dignity.

Civilian Impact

Digital warfare targets civilian infrastructure‚Äîpower grids, hospitals, schools. Weaponized disinformation campaigns undermine democratic discourse and social cohesion. The distinction between combatants and civilians becomes blurred in cyberspace.

The Peace Imperative

This crisis demands immediate action to establish international norms for digital peace. We must cease current digital warfare and prevent future escalation through diplomatic dialogue, binding agreements, and technological frameworks that serve human dignity rather than destruction.

Our Vision for the Summit

The Budapest Digital Peace Summit represents the culmination of our work to date and a launchpad for the future. We envision:

  • Immediate cessation of digital warfare in the Ukraine-Russia conflict
  • Binding international agreements preventing future cyber warfare escalation
  • Diplomatic frameworks for digital peace and conflict prevention
  • International norms prohibiting AI-driven autonomous weapons systems
  • Budapest Declaration establishing comprehensive digital peace principles
