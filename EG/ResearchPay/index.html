Executive Overview

The Problem

Clinical research faces a $10B annual problem: 80% of trials fail to meet enrollment deadlines, with payment friction being a primary cause. Traditional payment methods take 6-8 weeks, deterring participation from underserved communities who need immediate compensation.

Our Solution

ResearchPay enables instant, secure payments to research participants through E-Group's integrated technology stack. Participants receive compensation immediately after visit completion, with full regulatory compliance and audit trails.

Market Impact

By removing payment barriers, ResearchPay accelerates trial enrollment by 40%, reduces dropout rates by 35%, and increases diversity in clinical research by making trials accessible to financially constrained populations.

How ResearchPay Works

Participant Journey

1. Digital Enrollment

Participant enrolls in clinical trial and sets up MyD Wallet for secure identity verification and payment receipt.

2. Visit Completion

After completing study visit, coordinator verifies attendance through COIOS integrated system.

3. Instant Authorization

Payment authorization flows through FedX clearing system with real-time compliance checks.

4. Immediate Payment

Funds deposited instantly to participant's MyD Wallet or linked bank account via IOX payment rails.

5. Data Integration

Transaction data synchronized with InnoHealth DataLake for compliance reporting and analytics.
