Executive Overview

The Problem

Healthcare complexity costs the US $265B annually in administrative burden. Patients struggle with insurance navigation, appointment scheduling, medication management, and care coordination. 70% of patients report feeling overwhelmed by healthcare decisions.

Our Solution

Health Companion provides an AI-powered personal healthcare assistant that simplifies every aspect of the patient journey. Using advanced NLP and the InnoHealth DataLake, we deliver personalized, context-aware guidance 24/7.

Market Impact

By reducing administrative burden and improving care coordination, Health Companion saves patients 15 hours monthly, reduces missed appointments by 60%, and improves medication adherence by 45%, leading to better outcomes and lower costs.

How Health Companion Works

Patient Experience Journey

1. Secure Onboarding

Patient creates account via MyD Wallet with verified identity. Health records automatically imported from InnoHealth DataLake with consent.

2. AI Health Assessment

Advanced NLP analyzes medical history, current conditions, and preferences to create personalized health profile and care recommendations.

3. Intelligent Navigation

AI assistant provides real-time guidance for insurance questions, finding providers, scheduling appointments, and understanding treatments.

4. Proactive Management

Automated reminders for medications, appointments, and preventive care. Integration with COIOS for clinical trial matching when relevant.

5. Continuous Learning

System learns from interactions and outcomes, continuously improving personalization through InnoHealth DataLake analytics.
