Our Journey

From inspired vision to global impact - the evolution of DCF's mission to ensure technology serves humanity.

2017
Foundation of Vision
Cardinal Silvano M. Tomasi's international symposium "Prospects for a world free from nuclear weapons and integral disarmament" laid the groundwork for DCF's mission.
2020
DCF Formation
Domus Communis Foundation established under Cardinal Tomasi's patronage to advance ethical technology development globally.
2023
Global Expansion
DCF constellation model launched with operations in USA, Hungary, and Ghana, creating a global network for ethical AI development.
2024
Papal Recognition
Pope Francis's G7 address and subsequent statements reinforce DCF's mission to center human dignity in AI development.
2025
DCF Hungary Launch
Official launch of DCF Hungary as a nonprofit public benefit organization, establishing Central Europe's hub for ethical AI research and development.
