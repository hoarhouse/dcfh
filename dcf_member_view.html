About
Antal Kuthy is a Hungarian high-tech AI and Trust technology entrepreneur, founder, and CEO of E-Group (1993), an innovative science-led technology group based in Budapest. He is a telecommunications engineer who studied computer and information science at the Budapest University of Technology, later complementing his studies with European telecommunications and fields such as evolutionary and financial economics, and game theory at UCL, London.
Antal has worked in the US, UK, EU, Japan, and Hong Kong, Vietnam and is an active member of several European innovation organizations, including EIT Health and EIT Digital. He serves as Chair of the Smart Processing Data & AI pillar of EU's Important Project of Common European Interest on next generation Cloud and edge Infrastructure and Services Program (IPCEI-CIS).
