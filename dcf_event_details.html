Interactive workshop exploring practical ways to integrate Catholic Social Teaching principles into AI development workflows. Led by Dr. Sarah Johnson and Fr. AI Ethics Catholic Social Teaching Workshop Development Implementation

About This Workshop

This comprehensive workshop is designed for AI developers, researchers, and technology leaders who want to ensure their work aligns with Catholic Social Teaching principles. Participants will learn practical methodologies for implementing ethical frameworks in AI development, from initial design through deployment and monitoring.


The session combines theological insights with hands-on technical guidance, making it accessible to both technical and non-technical audiences. All participants will receive a comprehensive toolkit for ongoing ethical AI development.

What You'll Learn
