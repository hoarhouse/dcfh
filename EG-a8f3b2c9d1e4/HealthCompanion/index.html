Your sovereign AI health assistant. Your data. Your control.

Health Companion is a sovereign personal health AI that puts you in complete control of your medical data. We aggregate your scattered health records into a unified view, enable AI-powered health insights, and ensure your most sensitive information never leaves your control—all built on E-Group's proven privacy infrastructure.

Your health data is a fragmented mess. Lab results in one portal. Prescriptions in another. Imaging stored somewhere else. Insurance claims scattered across multiple systems. Getting a complete picture of your health requires manually collecting records from dozens of sources. And if you want AI to help analyze patterns?
