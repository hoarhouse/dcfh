Why Federated Architecture Is Required

This is impossible with centralized AI - federated learning is the ONLY architecture that works

Legal Impossibility

No licensing body can legally share member data with third parties. GDPR Article 9 prohibits processing of professional data without explicit consent. Cross-border data transfers face insurmountable legal barriers.

Political Impossibility

No EU state will cede control of professional licensing to a central authority. National sovereignty over professional standards is non-negotiable. Each country maintains unique qualification frameworks.

Commercial Impossibility

Would require 1000+ individual data sharing agreements across jurisdictions. Each agreement would take months of legal negotiation. Any single refusal breaks the entire system.
