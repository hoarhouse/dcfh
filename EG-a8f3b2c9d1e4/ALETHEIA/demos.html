Interactive Demonstrations

Three experiences that make ALETHEIA's value proposition tangible.

Credential Roulette

What it is

A game where you try to spot fake credentials.

How it works

You're shown 10 professional credentials from across Europe - some real, some fraudulent. You guess which is which. Most people score around 60%. Then you replay with ALETHEIA's network intelligence turned on, and watch detection jump to 95%+.

Why it sticks

It's fun, competitive, and the "aha moment" is undeniable - you physically experience the gap between human judgment and network intelligence.

The Labyrinth

What it is

A simulation of verifying credentials across three EU countries using today's process.

How it works

You play as an HR director verifying a doctor who trained in Spain, France, and Poland. You navigate three different government websites, in three languages, with different forms, fees, and delays. A timer shows 50+ days passing. Then ALETHEIA does the same thing in 3 seconds.

Why it sticks

Frustration is memorable. After 10 minutes of bureaucratic pain, the instant ALETHEIA verification feels like magic. Nobody forgets the contrast.

Shadow Network

What it is

A live intelligence dashboard showing credential patterns across Europe.

How it works

An interactive map and feed displaying verification demand by country, fraud alerts, workforce mobility flows, and regulatory changes. Data updates in real-time (simulated). Users can explore by profession, country, or time period.

Why it sticks

It looks and feels like real intelligence infrastructure. Prospects see themselves using it. It makes the "network effect" visible - the more nodes, the more insight.
