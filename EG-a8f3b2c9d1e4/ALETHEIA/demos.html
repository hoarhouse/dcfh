Why These Experiences Exist

ALETHEIA solves a problem most people don't know exists - until they experience it.

Cross-border credential verification in Europe is fundamentally broken. 27 countries. Thousands of licensing bodies. No unified system. Every verification is a manual odyssey through bureaucracy, language barriers, and siloed databases.

Meanwhile, credential fraud thrives in the gaps. Fake doctors practice for months. Credential mills issue thousands of worthless degrees. No single authority can see the patterns because no single authority has the full picture.

Federated learning changes this. But "federated learning" is abstract. "Algorithm travels to data instead of data traveling to algorithm" sounds like tech jargon.
