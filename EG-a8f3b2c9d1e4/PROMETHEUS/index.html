Why Federated Architecture Is Required

This is impossible with centralized carbon accounting‚Äîfederated learning is the ONLY architecture that works

Legal Impossibility

GDPR and trade secret laws prevent suppliers from sharing detailed production data. Energy consumption patterns, process efficiency, and material sourcing reveal competitive intelligence. No legal framework exists for centralizing this data.

Commercial Impossibility

Competitors often share the same suppliers. Centralizing production data would expose one company's supply chain efficiency to its rivals. No supplier will put actual production data in a customer-controlled platform.

Scale Impossibility

Manual supplier surveys achieve 20-30% response rates with unreliable data. A typical enterprise has 5,000+ tier-1 suppliers and 50,000+ in deeper tiers. Questionnaire-based approaches cannot scale.
