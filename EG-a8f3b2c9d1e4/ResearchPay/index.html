Your health data stored in YOUR vault. Location: YOUR choice (EU-hosted, on-premise, multi-region). Encryption: AES-256, only YOU hold keys. Already proven: Managing 100K patient records (Semmelweis Biobank). Data aggregation: Pulls from hospitals, labs, wearables into YOUR vault.

Step 3: Browse & Opt-In

See available studies: "Diabetes Prevention - €200/year". Review what they'll query: "Glucose levels, exercise data, diet logs". Understand compensation: "€15 per query, estimated 12 queries/year". One-tap opt-in (revoke anytime). Control: You decide which studies, which data, for how long.

COIOS + FedX

Researchers submit queries. COIOS translates to plain English: "Study asking: Do patients like you respond to metformin?" FedX executes query AT your vault (data never leaves). ▼

Step 1: Define Study Criteria

Inclusion criteria: Age 40-70, pre-diabetic, baseline glucose data. Required data fields: Fasting glucose, HbA1c, exercise logs. Compensation offered: €15 per query. Platform estimates: "723 patients match your criteria". Budget calculator: 723 × €15 = €10,845 total cost.

Step 2: Recruit Participants

Platform shows study to matching patients. Patients see: Study title, institution, compensation, what's queried. Patients opt-in if interested. Enrollment tracked: "4,247 of 5,000 target enrolled". Recruitment speed: Weeks not months (vs. traditional 6-18 months).

FedX Platform

Step 3: Execute Federated Queries

Submit research question: "What % show glucose improvement after 6 months?" Query travels TO patient vaults (data doesn't move). Homomorphic encryption: Math runs on encrypted data. Differential privacy: Mathematical guarantee of anonymity. Queries execute in parallel across network.

Step 4: Receive Aggregate Results

Results return: "723 patients queried, 73% showed improvement". Aggregate statistics only: Average reduction 12.4 mg/dL. Breakdown: Exercise only (58%), Diet only (61%), Both (84%). ZERO patient identities revealed. ZERO raw records downloaded. Export: Aggregate data for publication. Proof: 723 patients each earned €15.
