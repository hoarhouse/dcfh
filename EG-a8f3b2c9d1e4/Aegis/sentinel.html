How It Works

Five simple steps from installation to collective protection

1

Download & Deploy

  • 50MB lightweight agent
  • Windows Server, Linux, or container deployment
  • 15-minute installation with guided setup
  • No hardware required
2

Baseline Learning

  • Agent observes your network for 48-72 hours
  • Builds ML model of "normal" for YOUR environment
  • No two organizations have the same baseline
  • Learns continuously as your network evolves
3

Threat Detection

  • Compares real-time activity against baseline
  • Matches patterns against collective threat intelligence
  • Flags anomalies with confidence scoring
  • Zero false positive tuning over time
4

Anonymous Contribution

  • Suspicious patterns converted to mathematical fingerprints
  • All identifying information stripped automatically
  • Fingerprint sent to Argus coordinator
  • Your identity is never attached
5

Collective Protection

  • Your fingerprint helps identify emerging threats
  • Threat Lab researchers analyze patterns
  • New detection rules created
  • Rules pushed to ALL Sentinel installations
  • Everyone protected within hours
