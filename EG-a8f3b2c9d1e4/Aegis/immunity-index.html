Herd Immunity for Cybersecurity

COVID taught everyone what herd immunity means - the same principle applies to cybersecurity.

When 30% of a sector participates in federated defense, the entire sector benefits.

Attacks that hit one organization trigger protection for all others within hours.

Sequential attacks become impossible - attackers lose the element of surprise.

The more organizations participate, the faster threats are neutralized.

"Ransomware gangs attack hundreds of organizations with the same exploit over weeks. With herd immunity, organization #3 getting hit means organizations #4-200 are already protected."
