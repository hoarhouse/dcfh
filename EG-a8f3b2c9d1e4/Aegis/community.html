Herd Immunity for Cybersecurity

The Core Insight

COVID taught everyone about herd immunity. Ransomware is a pandemic. When 30% of a sector participates in federated defense, attacks that hit one organization are blocked across all participants within hours. The mathematics are identical‚Äîcollective protection emerges from distributed participation.

The Problem

Organizations fight alone. The same attacks hit thousands of companies sequentially, like dominoes falling one by one. When organization #3 gets breached, organizations #4 through #200 have no warning, no protection, no chance. Attackers exploit this isolation, reusing the same tactics across entire industries.

The Solution

Collective defense where every participant strengthens everyone else. No sharing of sensitive data, no exposure of vulnerabilities‚Äîjust mathematical patterns that protect the whole network. Like antibodies spreading through a population, threat intelligence propagates instantly to all participants.

2
