AI Ethics Curriculum for Catholic Schools

Creating comprehensive K-12 curriculum materials that teach AI ethics grounded in Catholic Social Teaching

Status
Active
Team Size
12 Members
Location
North America
Timeline
6 Months
This project aims to develop comprehensive curriculum materials for K-12 Catholic schools that teach AI ethics through the lens of Catholic Social Teaching. We're creating age-appropriate lesson plans, interactive exercises, and assessment tools that help students understand the moral implications of artificial intelligence while grounding their learning in principles of human dignity, common good, and social justice.

Project Overview

65% Complete

Project Goals

  • Develop age-appropriate AI ethics curriculum for grades K-12
  • Integrate Catholic Social Teaching principles into lesson plans
  • Create interactive exercises and case studies
  • Establish assessment rubrics for ethical reasoning
  • Pilot program in 10 Catholic schools across North America
  • Publish open-source materials for global adoption

Current Phase

We are currently in the development phase, focusing on creating lesson plans for middle school students (grades 6-8). The elementary curriculum foundation has been completed, and we're now working on more complex ethical scenarios that are appropriate for adolescent cognitive development.
