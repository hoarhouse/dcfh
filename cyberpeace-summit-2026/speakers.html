Who Will Be There

The summit brings together diverse perspectives essential for addressing the complex challenges at the intersection of faith, technology, and peace.

Faith Leaders

Cardinals, bishops, theologians, and religious leaders from Catholic and other faith traditions providing moral guidance and spiritual wisdom for navigating technological challenges.

Technology Experts

AI researchers, cybersecurity professionals, software engineers, and technology ethicists who understand the technical realities and possibilities of emerging technologies.

Policy Makers

Government officials, diplomats, legislative advisors, and international organization representatives who can translate principles into actionable policies and regulations.

Academic Researchers

Scholars from philosophy, political science, international relations, and peace studies who bring rigorous analysis and historical perspective to contemporary challenges.

Civil Society Leaders

Representatives from NGOs, peace organizations, and advocacy groups working on the ground to promote digital rights and peaceful technology use.

Military Ethics Experts

Current and former military officials, defense analysts, and just war theorists who understand the realities of modern warfare and security challenges.

Youth Representatives

Young technologists, activists, and faith leaders who will inherit the digital world we're shaping and bring fresh perspectives on technology's role in society.
