Cancellation & Refund Policy

Cancellation Schedule

  • Before December 31, 2025: Full refund minus ¬25 processing fee
  • January 1-31, 2026: 50% refund
  • After January 31, 2026: No refund, but registration can be transferred

Transfer Policy

Registrations can be transferred to another person from the same organization at no charge up to 7 days before the event.

Force Majeure

In case of event cancellation due to circumstances beyond our control, registrants will be offered the choice of:

  • Full refund of registration fees
  • Transfer to virtual participation
  • Credit toward future DCF Hungary events

Virtual Registration

Virtual registrations are non-refundable but provide immediate access to summit content once available.
