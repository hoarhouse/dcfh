About the Summit

The Cyberpeace Summit 2026 is the first global Catholic conference addressing the ethical challenges of artificial intelligence in warfare, cyber threats to nuclear systems, and the path to digital peace. It brings together faith leaders, technologists, policymakers, and academics for critical dialogue on humanity's digital future.

The summit will take place on February 20, 2026, in Budapest, Hungary, organized by Digital Christian Fellowship Hungary in collaboration with Vatican institutions and international partners. This historic gathering aims to establish moral frameworks for emerging technologies that threaten global stability.

Budapest was selected for several strategic reasons:

  • Central Location: Easily accessible from both Western and Eastern Europe, symbolizing bridge-building between different perspectives
  • Historical Significance: A city that has experienced both conflict and reconciliation, providing powerful context for peace discussions
  • Strong Catholic Heritage: Hungary's deep Catholic roots and active faith community provide supportive environment
  • Excellent Infrastructure: World-class conference facilities, hotels, and transportation systems
  • DCF Hungary Base: Local organizing committee ensures smooth operations and cultural integration

The summit is designed for diverse stakeholders concerned about the intersection of technology, ethics, and peace:

  • Faith Leaders: Clergy, theologians, and religious educators seeking to understand and address technological challenges
  • Technology Professionals: AI researchers, cybersecurity experts, and engineers committed to ethical technology
  • Policy Makers: Government officials, diplomats, and legislative staff working on technology governance
  • Academics: Researchers in ethics, international relations, peace studies, and technology policy
  • Civil Society: NGO leaders, peace activists, and human rights advocates
  • Students: Future leaders interested in the ethical dimensions of technology

Virtual attendance options make the summit accessible to global participants who cannot travel to Budapest.

The summit will address four core themes:

  • Autonomous Weapons Systems: The ethics of lethal AI, meaningful human control, and international governance frameworks
  • Nuclear Cyber Security: Vulnerabilities in nuclear command systems, AI in nuclear decision-making, and preventing accidental war
  • AI-Driven Conflict: Speed of algorithmic warfare, AI-powered disinformation, and escalation dynamics
  • Digital Peace Frameworks: Catholic social teaching applied to technology, international cooperation models, and implementation strategies

Each theme will be explored through keynote speeches, expert panels, working groups, and interactive workshops designed to produce actionable recommendations.

Registration

Registration will officially open in early 2025. Currently, you can view the registration options and pricing on our registration page. The process will be:

  • Select your registration type (Virtual, Standard, or VIP)
  • Complete the online registration form
  • Submit payment through our secure payment processor
  • Receive confirmation email with next steps

Early bird pricing (20% discount) is available for registrations completed before December 31, 2025. Group discounts are available for 5+ participants from the same organization.

Virtual Attendance (Free):

  • Live stream access to all plenary sessions
  • Digital summit materials
  • Q&A participation via chat
  • 30-day access to recordings

In-Person Standard (¬299):

  • Full access to all sessions and working groups
  • Summit materials and conference bag
  • Daily lunch and coffee breaks
  • Networking events access
  • Certificate of participation

In-Person VIP (¬999):

  • Everything in Standard tier PLUS:
  • Private dinner with speakers
  • Exclusive pre-summit workshop
  • Reserved front seating
  • VIP lounge access
  • Direct speaker access during breaks
  • Signed summit publication

We understand plans can change. Our refund policy is:

  • Before December 31, 2025: Full refund minus ¬25 processing fee
  • January 1-31, 2026: 50% refund
  • After January 31, 2026: No refund, but registration can be transferred to another person

Registrations can be transferred to someone else from your organization at no charge up to 7 days before the event. In case of summit cancellation due to force majeure, full refunds will be provided.

Yes, we offer special discounts to make the summit accessible:

  • Students: 50% discount on in-person registration with valid student ID
  • Clergy: 50% discount for ordained clergy and religious
  • Group Rates: 20% discount for groups of 5 or more from the same organization
  • Developing Nations: Special rates available upon request
  • Scholarships: Limited full scholarships available for qualifying participants

Contact registration@cyberpeacesummit2026.org for discount codes or scholarship applications.

Sponsorship

We welcome sponsors who align with our mission of digital peace and ethical technology. To become a sponsor:

  • Review sponsorship tiers on our sponsorship page
  • Submit an inquiry form or contact sponsors@cyberpeacesummit2026.org
  • Our development team will schedule a call to discuss your goals
  • We'll create a sponsorship agreement tailored to your needs
  • Upon agreement, you'll receive a sponsor toolkit with logos and guidelines

Sponsorship levels range from ¬10,000 (Bronze) to ¬100,000+ (Platinum), with custom packages available.

Sponsorship provides multiple benefits depending on your level:

  • Visibility: Logo placement on materials, website, and summit venues
  • Thought Leadership: Speaking opportunities at higher tiers
  • Networking: VIP passes and exclusive events access
  • Brand Alignment: Association with ethical AI and digital peace
  • Media Coverage: Inclusion in press releases and media materials
  • Direct Engagement: Exhibition space and participant interaction

All sponsors receive a detailed impact report showing the summit's outcomes and your contribution's role.

Yes, higher-tier sponsors have program participation opportunities:

  • Platinum Sponsors: Keynote speaking slot (15-20 minutes)
  • Gold Sponsors: Panel participation opportunity
  • All Sponsors: Can propose workshop topics for consideration

Speaking content must align with summit themes and undergo review to ensure quality and relevance. Sponsors cannot use speaking slots for direct product promotion but can share thought leadership and expertise.

Sponsorship deadlines vary by benefit inclusion:

  • October 31, 2025: Deadline for inclusion in printed materials
  • December 15, 2025: Deadline for speaking opportunity consideration
  • January 15, 2026: Final deadline for website and digital inclusion

We recommend committing early as Platinum (limit 2) and Gold (limit 4) sponsorships are limited. Bronze and Silver sponsorships remain available until January 2026 or until capacity is reached.

Logistics & Travel

The specific venue in Budapest is being finalized and will be announced by mid-2025. We are securing a premier conference center that meets our requirements:

  • Central Budapest location for easy access
  • Capacity for 500+ participants with breakout rooms
  • Full accessibility features
  • State-of-the-art AV and streaming capabilities
  • On-site catering facilities

Once confirmed, detailed venue information including address, maps, and transportation options will be provided on our venue page.

We are negotiating special rates with hotels near the venue. Options will include:

  • Official Summit Hotels: Special negotiated rates for participants
  • Budget Options: Hostels and guesthouses for students and budget-conscious attendees
  • Luxury Hotels: Five-star options for VIP participants
  • Religious Houses: Some religious communities may offer accommodation

Hotel booking information with special codes will be sent to registered participants. We recommend booking early as February is a popular time for Budapest conferences.

Visa requirements depend on your nationality:

  • EU/EEA Citizens: No visa required, just valid ID or passport
  • Schengen Visa-Free Countries: Many countries have visa-free access for up to 90 days
  • Visa Required: Citizens of some countries need to apply for a Schengen visa

We will provide official invitation letters for visa applications upon registration. Please check visa requirements immediately after registering and apply at least 6-8 weeks before travel. Contact registration@cyberpeacesummit2026.org if you need visa support documentation.

The summit is a full-day event on February 20, 2026. Preliminary schedule:

  • 8:00-9:00 AM: Registration and welcome coffee
  • 9:00-9:30 AM: Opening ceremony and prayer
  • 9:30-10:30 AM: Keynote addresses
  • 10:45 AM-12:30 PM: Morning working groups
  • 12:30-2:00 PM: Lunch and networking
  • 2:00-3:30 PM: Panel discussions
  • 3:45-5:15 PM: Afternoon workshops
  • 5:30-6:30 PM: Closing plenary and commitments
  • 7:00 PM: Optional VIP dinner (VIP tickets only)

A detailed program will be released in December 2025. Virtual participants can join all plenary sessions and selected workshops via livestream.
