About This Resource

This comprehensive guide provides Catholic institutions with a structured framework for implementing AI governance that aligns with Catholic Social Teaching principles. The document covers ethical considerations, practical implementation strategies, and provides tools for decision-making processes.

The framework addresses key challenges faced by Catholic institutions when adopting AI technologies, including maintaining human dignity, ensuring transparency, protecting privacy, and promoting the common good. It includes case studies from Catholic universities, healthcare systems, and dioceses that have successfully implemented AI governance structures.

Key sections include:

  • Theological foundations for AI ethics in Catholic institutions
  • Practical governance structures and committees
  • Risk assessment and mitigation strategies
  • Implementation timeline and milestones
  • Evaluation and continuous improvement processes
