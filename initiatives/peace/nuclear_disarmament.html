Nuclear Disarmament & AI Governance

Preventing the integration of artificial intelligence into nuclear weapons systems and establishing transparent, accountable governance frameworks for military AI technologies worldwide.

View Treaty Progress Our Impact

The Critical Challenge

Nuclear weapons represent humanity's greatest existential threat. The prospect of integrating artificial intelligence into these systems‚Äîremoving human oversight from decisions of ultimate consequence‚Äîrepresents a new level of global risk that demands immediate, coordinated action.

DCF's Nuclear Disarmament Initiative, launched in partnership with the Vatican's Pontifical Council for Justice and Peace, works to prevent AI-nuclear integration while advocating for comprehensive nuclear disarmament based on Catholic Social Teaching principles of human dignity and global solidarity.

The Window is Closing

Military AI spending is projected to reach $18.6 billion by 2025. Several nuclear powers are already researching AI integration into command and control systems. We have perhaps five years to establish binding international frameworks before AI-nuclear integration becomes widespread and irreversible.

Our comprehensive approach combines diplomatic advocacy, technical research, and ethical framework development. We work directly with nuclear powers, UN agencies, and civil society organizations to establish binding international agreements that prevent AI-nuclear integration while advancing broader nuclear disarmament goals.

Grounded in the Vatican's teaching that nuclear weapons are immoral, our initiative represents the most comprehensive faith-based approach to nuclear disarmament in the AI era, bringing together Catholic institutions worldwide in service of global peace and security.
