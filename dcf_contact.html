Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about DCF Hungary and our mission.

How can I become a member of DCF Hungary? ‚ñº
Membership is open to researchers, developers, academics, policy makers, and anyone committed to ethical AI development. Visit our membership page or contact us to learn about the application process and membership benefits.
What kind of collaboration opportunities are available? ‚ñº
We collaborate on research projects, educational programs, policy development, and technology initiatives. Partnerships range from academic research to industry collaboration and international development projects focused on ethical AI.
Do I need to be Catholic to participate in DCF Hungary? ‚ñº
No, absolutely not. While our work is inspired by Catholic Social Teaching principles, we welcome and serve people of all faiths and backgrounds. Our focus is on shared human values and ethical technology development.
How can my organization partner with DCF Hungary? ‚ñº
We're interested in partnerships with universities, research institutions, tech companies, NGOs, and government agencies. Contact us to discuss collaboration opportunities that align with our mission of ethical AI development.
What is DCF Hungary's connection to the Vatican? ‚ñº
DCF operates under the patronage of His Eminence Cardinal Silvano Maria Tomasi and aligns with papal teachings on ethical technology. However, we function as an independent nonprofit organization focused on practical AI ethics implementation.
