About DCF Hungary

We are a global foundation dedicated to ensuring artificial intelligence and emerging technologies are developed and deployed in ways that honor human dignity, promote justice, and serve the common good of all humanity.

Our Foundation

Founded on the principles of Catholic Social Teaching, DCF Hungary brings together researchers, technologists, ethicists, and advocates from around the world. We believe that technology should amplify human potential rather than replace human connection, and that artificial intelligence must be developed with careful consideration of its impact on vulnerable populations and global communities.

Our work spans research, education, policy development, and practical implementation of ethical frameworks that guide responsible innovation. We collaborate with universities, healthcare institutions, government agencies, and technology companies to ensure that human dignity remains at the center of technological advancement.
